WELCOME TO Meditro TALK

Meditro Talk is an app that helps create a revolution in technology and education with innovation in the world of technology and rapid education.

Meditro Talk is a revolutionary app designed to bridge communication barriers and enhance the lives of individuals within the deaf and blind community.

Our mission is to provide a comprehensive platform that facilitates education and seamless interaction, destroying barriers that hinder effective communication.

Our specialties and examples of the problems we work on

Our specialties talkmeditro is a multidisciplinary application equipped with the latest educational methods that teach deaf and mute children and adults, and includes a wide range of fields that benefit deaf and mute people in facilitating their lives. In all fields and stages of education, work and treatments, education is carried out at the hands of experts from teachers and doctors who are proficient in dealing with sign language, which was created on the philosophical edifice for the deaf and dumb, which works to attract attention and focus to ensure greater benefit from me at the hands of specialists in In dealing with sign language.

Accessing items and images

Deaf and mute people may find it difficult to access items, companies and images if they are not functional in a format that can be easily understood by those with vision challenges..

Lack of awareness and understanding about their needs

Deaf and mute people may face challenges in providing the necessary support and understanding from society towards their unique needs.

ifficulties in obtaining emergency information

In emergency and crisis situations, it may be difficult for deaf and mute people to access important information and safety instructions due to the inability to use audio alarms.
